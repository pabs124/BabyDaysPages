Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 2, 2026 · Last updated: May 2, 2026

Baby Days is built by Sahil Pabby ("we," "us," "I"). This policy explains how the Baby Days iOS app handles your information. The short version: your voice recordings never leave your device, and we don't run analytics, ads, or tracking of any kind.

1. Who we are

Baby Days is an independent app developed and operated by Sahil Pabby. You can reach me at pabs642@gmail.com for any privacy question or request.

2. What the app handles

Baby Days lets you record diary entries, attach photos, track milestones, and create child profiles. The data the app touches falls into these categories:

  • Voice recordings. Audio you record from inside the app.
  • Transcripts. Text generated from your audio by Apple's on-device speech recognition.
  • Photos. Images you choose to attach to entries or milestones, from your photo library.
  • Child profiles. The name, date of birth, and avatar color you choose for each child.
  • Milestones and entries. Anything you type, mark, or save inside the app.
  • Purchase status. Whether you've purchased Baby Days Pro, supplied by Apple's StoreKit.

3. Where your data lives

On your device

Everything you create — recordings, transcripts, photos, milestones, profiles — is stored locally on your iPhone in the app's sandboxed storage.

In your private iCloud

If you have iCloud enabled on your device, the app syncs your data to your own private CloudKit container (iCloud.com.sahilpabby.babydays) so it's available across your devices and recoverable if you lose your phone. This is your iCloud, not ours. I cannot read your data. Apple, not Baby Days, controls access. If you delete your iCloud data or disable iCloud for the app, the synced copy is removed by Apple.

Speech recognition

Transcription runs on your device using Apple's SFSpeechRecognizer with the requiresOnDeviceRecognition flag set. Audio is not sent to Apple's servers, to me, or to any third party for transcription.

Purchases

The one-time Baby Days Pro purchase is processed by Apple via the App Store and StoreKit. Apple receives the transaction; I receive an entitlement that says "this Apple ID has purchased Pro." I do not see your Apple ID, payment method, or billing details. Apple's privacy policy applies to their handling of the transaction.

4. What I do not collect

Baby Days has no servers of its own. There are no accounts to create, no logins, no profiles on a Baby Days backend. Specifically:

  • No third-party analytics SDKs (no Firebase, no Mixpanel, no anything).
  • No advertising SDKs.
  • No tracking across apps or websites. The app does not request the IDFA.
  • No location collection.
  • No contact list access.
  • No crash-reporting service. (Apple may collect anonymous diagnostics if you opted into "Share with App Developers" in iOS Settings; that data goes through Apple, not directly to me.)

5. Sharing

I do not sell, rent, or share your data with anyone. Period.

The app provides features that you can choose to use to share specific entries — for example, sending a single entry via iMessage, WhatsApp, or email; or exporting a PDF book. When you do, the destination app's privacy policy applies to that copy.

6. Children's privacy

Baby Days is designed for parents and caregivers to record memories about their own children. The app is not directed at children, and children do not create accounts or interact directly with any Baby Days service. Because the app stores everything in the parent's own device and iCloud, and because there is no Baby Days backend, no personal information about a child is transmitted to me. If you believe data has been collected from a child in a way this policy does not cover, contact me at pabs642@gmail.com and I will respond promptly.

7. Permissions the app requests

  • Microphone — required to record voice entries. You can revoke this in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Microphone at any time.
  • Speech Recognition — required to transcribe recordings on-device. Revoke in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Speech Recognition.
  • Photo Library — optional. Only used if you choose to attach a photo. Revoke in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Photos.
  • Notifications — optional. Used only for the reminders feature if you enable it.

8. Your rights and how to delete your data

Your data is yours. You can delete it any time:

  • Delete a single entry — swipe to delete in the timeline.
  • Delete a child profile and everything in it — open the profile, tap Delete.
  • Delete everything — uninstall the app from your iPhone, then go to iOS Settings → Apple ID → iCloud → Manage Account Storage → Baby Days → Delete Data.

Because I have no copy of your data, I cannot delete it for you — but I also cannot retain it after you do the above. If you have any difficulty, email pabs642@gmail.com and I'll walk you through it.

Depending on where you live (e.g., the EU/UK under GDPR, California under CCPA), you may have specific rights to access, correct, or delete personal information. Because Baby Days does not hold a copy of your personal information on any server, the most direct way to exercise these rights is the deletion steps above. If you need a written confirmation, email me.

9. Security

Data on your device is protected by iOS file encryption tied to your device passcode. Data in iCloud is protected by Apple's CloudKit security model. Network calls (only to Apple-controlled endpoints, e.g., StoreKit) use HTTPS. There is no Baby Days server to breach because there is no Baby Days server.

10. Changes to this policy

If this policy changes meaningfully, I'll update the "Last updated" date at the top and, where appropriate, surface a notice in the app. Continued use after a change means you've accepted the updated policy.

11. Contact

Questions, concerns, or requests: pabs642@gmail.com
